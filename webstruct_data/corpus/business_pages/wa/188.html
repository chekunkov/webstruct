Executive Visions Inc. (EVI) is a Primetime Emmy winning Producer, corporate Event Management Agency and Production Company of Brand-Immersive Experiences™.  EVI produces corporate communications, notable live events, entertainment and television programming for some of the world's foremost companies. EVI creates and designs integrated programs that feature celebrity entertainment as a vehicle for corporate brand building.

Solutions include strategy development, creative design, staging, production and management of:
celebrity entertainment, concert booking, award shows & events, broadcast television, corporate meetings, event marketing & production, product launches, show tours, training programs, marketing communications, special event planning, & related multi-media.

EVI's corporate communications services are delivered in all forms of media including:
live events, award shows, high-definition TV (HDTV), film, video, DVD, print, environments, exhibits, trade shows, pavillions, displays and the internet.

Since 1986, EVI has specialized in corporate event planning, event production and project event management by delivering expert integration of the major business disciplines. The end-results enable our clients to better inform, entertain, sell to, train, motivate, and reward their target audiences. JCPENNEY JAM - CONCERT FOR AMERICA'S KIDS

Aired on CBS Television in Primetime

Created & Produced by EVI

WHAT:  JCPenney Jam is an industry first, cross-media initiative built around the biggest names in music in nearly every genre.  This live music mega event, concert and integrated marketing platform marks the largest project ever undertaken in America to raise awareness of the need for quality afterschool programs and funds so more working families have access to these programs.  Championed by theJCPenney Afterschool Fund a non-profit 501 (c) (3) organization committed to providing safe and enriching afterschool programs for America's 14.3 million children in the U.S. unsupervised between 3 - 6 p.m.

HOW:  EVI used a broad mix of communication channels to deliver powerful messaging about the contemporary JCPenney.  More than a one-time event, JCPenney Jam is an “ongoing” platform, strategically linked to the long-term business plan and touching all aspects of the company; from merchandising, advertising and promotion, to online initiatives, live events and PR.  More than 200 million emotionally-charged consumer impressions have been made, while clearly positioning JCPenney and the JCPenney Afterschool Fund as socially responsible leaders.

WHO:  The multi-genre line-up of music icons includes legendary vocal tenor Andrea Bocelli, country superstars Kenny ChesneyMartina McBride and Sara Evans rock icons Sting Jon Bon Jovi and rock super-group 3 Doors Down Latin pop star Alejandro Sanz multi-platinum artists Mary J. Blige and John Legend American Idol runner-up Katharine McPhee gifted trumpeter Chris Botti young vocalist Renee Oldstead and singer-pianist sensation Peter Cincotti. Hosted by entertainment powerhouses Dr. Phil and Robin McGraw musically directed by renowned producer David Foster backed by a celebrity rhythm section and a 64-piece symphony conducted by William Ross.

BROADCAST DATE:  Aired on the CBS Television Network as a Primetime Special.  The concert was taped by EVI at Los Angeles' historic Shrine Auditorium with 21 high-definition cameras in progressive HD format.

ONLINE:  Everything about the JAM, including videos from the concert, was integrated into a full website at AOL MUSIC.

DVD/CD:  This special 2-disc CONCERT DVD & AUDIO CD twin pack was sold exclusively at JCP.com and JCPenney stores with all profits going to the JCPenney Afterschool Fund. The concert DVD was digitally mastered by EVI in High Definition, Widescreen 16:9, Dolby Digital 5.1, Surround Sound, plus includes The Making of, Artist portraits, Afterschool Fund, Concert photo gallery.

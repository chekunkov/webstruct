About us

V1 Project Accounting, formerly ProjectMinder, is the ideal web-based project accounting solution for architects, engineers, surveyors, IT consultants, management consultants, marketing agencies, and other time-based project centric organisations.

Quick and easy to deploy, V1 Project Accounting is a comprehensive ‘out of the box’ system which is scalable and easy to use, removing the need for multiple spreadsheets and systems.  It enables project-oriented organisations to improve and simplify the way in which projects are costed, planned, managed and billed, helping balance cost efficiency with client satisfaction.

From project and resource planning, to time recording and costing, to accounting and billing, V1 Project Accounting provides all the tools needed to successfully deliver your projects on time and on budget.

V1 Project Accounting is part of V1 Limited.

About V1 Limited

V1 Ltd (“V1”) is a global provider of business automation software that enables both public and private sector organisations to streamline their business processes.

V1’s award-winning document management solution integrates into all major accounting and enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems, enabling the automated delivery, storage, management and processing of documents. 

Thousands of organisations worldwide are using V1’s document management software to automate their business processes, cut costs, free-up administration time and reduce paper consumption while enjoying a typical payback of just six months.

V1’s recently expanded product suite includes: facilities management, document management, professional services automation, project accounting, and learning & training management software.

V1 prides itself on its innovative solutions, personal and straightforward approach and dependable service.

V1 Ltd is anAdvanced Computer Software Group plc company.

Innux Technologies, Lda

Because innovating is LEADING,

we develop the solutions to your needs.

 

Innux Technologies is one of the leading Portuguese companies in the development of human resource management related solutions. We  work hard to provide our customers, which come from very diverse sectors of activity, the solutions designed to answer their needs regarding the management of their employees, by allying our know-how with the most advanced technologies.

For this purpose, we rely on strategic partners for the implementation and technical support of the software and hardware solutions developed.

With a strengthened national strategy, we decided to implement a new business area in the national market – the control and security division, especially dedicated to the segments of IP and analog CCTV surveillance, detection of toxic and explosive gases and also key management and control.

Innux combines strategic, technical, operational and organizational skills to a vast experience.  We analyze, plan and build solutions that produce fast results and create solid structures for the future. We initially identify our customers' needs, upraising and considering their options, before advancing to the design and implementation stage of our products, in such a way that the defined processes and architectures will create added value to their businesses.

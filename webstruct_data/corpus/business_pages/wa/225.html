About DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry

Providing St. Louis with children's dentistry, implant dentistry and cosmetic dentistry

At DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry, we want all of our patients to feel welcome and at ease. Our friendly staff is open and eager to answer your questions and make sure your visit with us is efficient and positive.

Our comfortable clinic is conveniently located at 1391 Smizer Mill Road in Fenton -- just off Highway 141. We welcome drop-ins and new patients, making our services as accessible as possible for the greater community.

A wide range of modern dental services allow us to serve you well, making sure that your family's oral health is the top priority at DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry.

Visit children’s dentistry specialist Dr. Jen to ensure ongoing oral health for all the little ones in your family. Bring the confidence back to your smile with the help of comfortable dental implants. From teeth whitening to caps and veneers we have a cosmetic dentistry solution to suit your smile.

At DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry, here are just a few of the ways your Fenton dentist makes your dental visits easy:

Convenient Location

Conveniently located at 1391 Smizer Mill Road in Fenton Missouri DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry is easy to find with plenty of free parking.

Emergency Service

We understand that accidents happen, and sometimes you need emergency dental care. At DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry, we’re available to you for dental emergencies.

Children's’ Play Area

Your kids will have a blast in our kiddie play area while they’re waiting for their dental appointment. There are toys and activities for everyone!

Flexible Payment Options

We accept cash, check, VISA, MasterCard, Discover, and interest-free payment plans through Care Credit.

Electronic Insurance Claims

Our friendly front office team processes all insurance claims electronically, which usually results in quick reimbursements from your insurance company.

No Dental Insurance? No Problem!

If you don’t have dental insurance, ask us about our Deco-Dental Membership Plan. It’s quick and easy to enroll, and ensures you and your family have access to the care you need.

We Accept New Patients

If you’ve come across our site and are looking for a new dentist, welcome! Thank you for considering DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry. We are accepting new patients, so please contact us today for more information. Our New Patient Forms can be found here.

Televisions

Kick back and relax in our operatories – they’re equipped with TVs for your viewing pleasure while we work on your teeth.

Contact DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry today to schedule an appointment or learn more about the services offered by your Fenton dentist.

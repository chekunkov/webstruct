About Florida Hospital

Florida Hospital is a not-for-profit organization that has served the Central Florida community since 1908 with a Mission to extend the healing ministry of Christ and a Vision to be a global pacesetter delivering preeminent faith-based health care. It is the oldest and largest health care system in Central Florida.

As the largest hospital in Florida and the busiest hospital in the U.S., Florida Hospital has 2,188 licensed beds on eight campuses in the greater Orlando area. With more than 16,700 staff members, Florida Hospital cares for 115,081 inpatients, 479,325 outpatients and 381,169 ER patients each year.

Florida Hospital is a leader in technology, procedures and patient care treating more heart, neurology, gynecology and vascular surgery patients than any other nationally recognized hospital. Florida Hospital has been recognized as one of America's Best Hospitals by U.S. News & World Report for 10 consecutive years.

About Orlando

Orlando is a hub for higher education and is home to numerous colleges and universities including University of Central Florida (UCF) Rollins College Florida Hospital College of Health Sciences and the UCF College of Medicine.

A new "medical city" is under development including the UCF College of Medicine, VA hospital, and Burnham Research Institute centering their locations in the southeast part of town near Lake Nona.

Greater Orlando is home to several renowned theme parks including Walt Disney World, Universal Studios Florida and Sea World. Because of Orlando's unique position directly in the center of Florida, both east (Cocoa, New Smyrna, Daytona) and west (Clearwater, St. Petersburg) coasts are one-hour trips and glorious weekend adventures.

There are numerous new home developments and many top rated schools throughout the Central Florida area. Opportunities are everywhere for a family to grow!

Our Campuses

There are eight Florida Hospital campuses in the Central Florida area (also known as the "Tri-County" area of Orange, Seminole, and Osceola counties). They range in size from a 50-bed community hospital in the north, to an over 1100-bed facility near downtown.

  • Florida Hospital Altamonte
  • Florida Hospital East Orlando
  • Florida Hospital Celebration Health
  • Florida Hospital Kissimmee
  • Winter Park Memorial Hospital
  • Florida Hospital for Children
  • Florida Hospital Apopka

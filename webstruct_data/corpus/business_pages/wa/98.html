Welcome Home, Seattle...

For Custom Homes, Additions, Kitchen and Bathroom Remodeling, Seattle Trusts Better Builders

Better Builders is Seattle’s Premier Green Building and Remodeling Company.  With 20 years and counting of construction experience under our belts, we are steadily establishing a sterling reputation throughout greater Seattle.  Our well-earned trust and stellar portfolio enables us to stand behind these guarantees:

  1. We will add the most possible value to your real estate investment or home
  2. We won’t be your lowest bid, but we won’t be your highest either

If finding the lowest bid is most important to you, then Better Builders may not be the right fit for your project.  However if…

  • You think your home deserves the best in quality products and expert workmanship…
  • You’re looking for master craftsmanship and the hallmarks of a headache-free building and remodeling experience: education, compassion, integrity and communication…

Then you are in the right place…. Welcome to Better Builders!

It is an honor and privilege to help our neighbors create the homes of their dreams - by building from the ground-up or re-imagining one room at a time.

The pages that follow are your invitation into the world of Better Builders.  You will meet the team, see our credentials, walk through our projects and hear directly from our clients in their own words.

We look forward to building something special with you.

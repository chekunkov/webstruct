Bunchball Advisory Board

Jason Blessing, Chief Executive Officer, Plex Systems

Jason Blessing is Chief Executive Officer of Plex Systems, Inc., the leader in cloud-based ERP software for manufacturers. Jason joined Plex Systems from Oracle, where he was senior vice president of application development. Previously, he held a variety of executive roles at Taleo, a pioneer in cloud-based talent management applications that was acquired by Oracle in 2011. At Taleo, he was executive vice president of products and technology, and in that role he managed the company’s entire product strategy, development and production. Prior to this position, he was group vice president and general manager of Taleo's small and medium business unit with global responsibility for sales, marketing, services and products. Before joining Taleo, Jason held senior management roles at Price Waterhouse, PeopleSoft, and Oracle. He holds a degree from the University of Michigan.

Jeffrey Diana, Chief People Officer, Atlassian

Jeffrey Diana is a builder of world class HR organizations. He recently was named chief people officer of development software provider Atlassian. Jeff’s focus is to enable the Australia-based company to scale its workforce globally. For the past two years, Jeff was Chief People Officer at SuccessFactors, which was acquired by SAP in February. There he built an HR organization from scratch, helping the company triple in size and establish a global footprint. He previously was chief HR officer at online travel giant Expedia and at Safeco Insurance, a $6.5 billion market leader. From 1997 to 2005, he held senior HR management roles at General Electric and Bellsouth. Jeff has master’s degrees in HR management and sociology from the University of South Carolina and a bachelor’s in American studies from Tufts.

Kelly O. Kay, Global Managing Partner, Software Practice, Heidrick & Struggles

Kelly Kay is the global managing partner of the Software Practice at Heidrick & Struggles, the world’s first leadership advisory firm. He conducts worldwide searches for technology companies, focusing on board, CEO and sales and marketing officers. Kelly also leads the firm’s Marketing, Sales & Strategy Officers Practice for technology companies on the West Coast. Previously, Kelly was a managing partner with a leading boutique retained executive search firm in Silicon Valley, and was a partner at the Center for Corporate Innovation (CCI), a nationwide CEO and CFO development, strategy and networking organization sponsored by McKinsey & Co. His career has also included successful stints as a senior executive at a variety of management consulting firms, in addition to sales and management roles at IBM. He has a bachelor’s degree in marketing from the University of San Francisco and a MBA from the Marshall School of Business at the University of Southern California.

Jesse Redniss, Chief Strategy Officer, Mass Relevance

Jesse Redniss is an Emmy-nominated entertainment executive and trendsetter of multi-screen, digital brand building, creative development and marketing. As Chief Strategy Officer at Mass Relevance, a pioneer of interactive social media marketing, Jesse develops, executes and aligns the company around strategic initiatives that anticipate client needs and move the industry forward.

Previously, Jesse served as the Senior Vice President of Digital for USA Network where he pioneered USA Network’s digital, social TV and second screen initiatives. He shifted online properties from a simple entertainment purpose to revenue-generating destinations with the help of Bunchball’s Nitro gamification platform. Under Jesse’s leadership, the USA Network digital team gained industry recognition through various awards, including two Shorty Awards, two AdAge Media Vanguard Awards, the 2008 Beacon Award and two Addy awards. Jesse also held positions at Tangible Media, Volatile Media and SONY 550DMV.

As the market leader and innovator in gamification, Bunchball’s gamification solutions have motivated more than 125 million users to complete more than 20 billion actions on behalf of over 300 customers. Now, you can harness the compelling power of gamification to increase employee engagement and customer loyalty with Bunchball Nitro, Nitro for Salesforce, Nitro for Remedyforce, Nitro for IBM Connections, Nitro for Jive, Nitro for NICE, and Nitro for SAP Jam.

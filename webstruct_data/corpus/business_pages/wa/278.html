Roof Replacement

Contact Disaster Kleenup Specialist for roof replacement in Monterey. If a major storm has swept through and knocked off tiles from your rooftop or a tree has fallen on your roof, it’s a good idea to contact us to evaluate the situation as soon as possible. As a home owner, you want to have the repair work done as early as possible to prevent additional damage and leaks from future storms. From repairing the foundation below the tiles, to working on the structure of your roof, count on Disaster Kleenup Specialists when your roof is in need of immediate repair.

Restoring Your Roof

If your roof has seen one too many storms, or if irreparable damage has occurred after a major storm has swept through, we provide roof replacement. If you roof is damaged beyond the point of repair, or if water damage has caused mold, or a weak or compromised roof, it might be a good idea to replace it, rather than have it repaired. If you have an older roof, replacing it could be the best option. Replacement can save you money in the long run because you can avoid paying for constant repairs to your old roof.

Emergency Repair

We offer a variety of roofing options to choose from including the best quality finish, color, and style. When a storm has compromised the quality of your roof, count on our roofers for timely service and emergency repair. Whether you need roof repair or replacement, don’t overpay or live with a damaged roof. When you are in need of immediate or emergency roofing services, you can rely on Disaster Kleenup Specialists for repair or roof replacement in Monterey.

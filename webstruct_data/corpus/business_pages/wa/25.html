Frequently Asked Questions

What office rental terms do you offer?
Peace Arch Business Center is built around being flexible to your needs. We provide officeconference room and seminar room rentals by the hour, day, or month. You can choose one of our Office Package Plans, or modify a plan to whatever fits your needs.

What is a virtual office?
Virtual offices are the same as renting one of our office suites, except you're not physically renting a office. You have the benefits of a physical corporate mailing address, dedicated phone number, personalized phone answering system and more. A virtual office is a great alternative to
home-based businesses and out of area sales representatives.
See our virtual office page for more details.

Do you have professional staffing?
Peace Arch Business Center is staffed with administrative assistants proficient with the latest office technologies. We provide additional services such as word processing copying printing scanning and notary service. We also have a full-time receptionist who will meet and greet your clients.

What do your receptionist services include?
Peace Arch Business Center receptionist services are available Monday - Friday from 9 am to 5 pm (after hours by appointment). Your receptionist will answer your calls with your personalized greeting and forward your calls to your email and/or voice mail. For our tenants your arriving clients are warmly greeted and made comfortable in our bright reception area until you are able to see them. And for arriving packages don't worry. You can go about your business and we'll receive and store them for you. We can also handle any out-going mail/packages and express shipping needs you may require.

Is there ample parking at your building?
Yes. we have plenty of free-outdoor parking for both tenants and visitors.

Is there public transportation to your offices?
Yes. Whatcom Transportation Authority (WTA) has a bus stop at the north end of Birch Bay Square. We are just a short, level walk to and from the covered bus stop. For the WTA bus schedule, please click here.

Are there restaurants or shops nearby?
Yes, we are located at Birch Bay Square Shopping Center that is directly off I-5 Exit 270. Know for national name-brand outlets, popular local boutiques, trendy cafés, home and garden shops, as well as specialty shops. Within a minutes walk you will be able to enjoy restaurants such as Bob's Burger & Brew, Jack in the Box, Wood's Coffee and The Deli at Birch Bay Market.

Points-based System

The points based system is where the UK immigration rules regulate migrants from outside the EEA. The points based system composes of five tiers.

Tier 1 (Entrepreneur) Visa

The Tier 1 (Entrepreneur) Visa sub category is available to applicants who wish to set up or take over a business in the UK. The investor must have access to at least £200,000 or access to least £50,000 from certain institutions as specified by the UKBA.

Tier 1 (Investor) Visa

Tier 2 (Investor) Visa sub category is available to applicants who wish to invest at least £1 million in the United Kingdom.

Tier 1 (Exceptional Talent) Visa

The Tier 1 (Exceptional Talent) sub category is available to those who are internationally recognized as exceptionally talented leaders world-wide or potential world leading talent in arts and science who wish to work in the UK.

Tier 2 Visa

The Tier 2 Visa applies to those individuals with a job offer from a UK based employer.

There are several Tier 2 Visa categories, these are:-

  • General
  • Minister of Religion
  • Sportspeople
  • Intra Company Transfer (ICT)

There are four sub-categories in the Intra Company transfer category, these are:-

  • Short-term staff – for established, skilled employees to be transferred to the UK based branch of their company for 12 months or less to fill a position that cannot be filled by a new recruit from the resident workforce.
  • Long-term staff – for established skilled employees to be transferred to the UK based branch of their company for more than 12 months to fill a post that cannot be filled by a new recruit from the resident workforce.
  • Graduate trainee – this channel allows the transfer of recent graduate employees to a UK branch of the same organisation as part of a structure graduate training programme which clearly defines progession towards a managerial or specialist role.
  • Skills transfer – this channel allows the transfer of new graduate employees to a UK branch of the same organisation to learn to skills and knowledge required to perform their job overseas or provide specialist skills or knowledge to the UK workforce.

Tier 4 Student Visa

Tier 4 Student Visas applies to those individual who wish to study in the UK. To qualify, applicants must have already been offered a position at an educational institution.

Tier 5 Visa

Tier 5 visas covers youth mobility and temporary workers. The Youth mobility scheme is open to the following countries: Australia, Canada, Japan, New Zealand, Monaco and Taiwan. An applicant must be 18 or over when his or her entry clearance becomes valid and under the age of 31 on the date of application is made.
The temporary workers sub-category must obtain a certificate of sponsorship before they can apply to work in the UK.

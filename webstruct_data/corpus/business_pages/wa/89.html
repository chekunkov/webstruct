Management Team

Jim Scullion, CEO

Jim Scullion joined Bunchball in May 2010. Jim has a proven track record of successfully growing early-stage and established businesses to profitability. Prior to joining Bunchball Jim served as CEO for two other Granite Ventures portfolio companies DecisionView and Tumbleweed Communications (where he also served as chairman). Jim also held the positions of CEO and Board member at Comnetix Inc. and has served in a number of executive positions at Identix including president and COO board member and CFO.

Rajat Paharia, Founder and Chief Product Officer

Widely recognized as the father of gamification, Rajat Paharia founded Bunchball in 2005. Since then, he has parlayed his unique understanding of technology and design – along with a preternatural ability to recognize patterns – into the creation of a company whose market-defining solutions have helped engage customers and motivate employees at a wide array of companies, including Toyota, Mattel, T-Mobile, Bravo, VMware, ESPN, BOX Technologies and Kimberly Clark. Before founding Bunchball, Rajat had built a technology design career at IDEO, Philips Consumer Electronics, IBM Research and ViewStar. Rajat earned a master’s degree in Computer Science (with a focus on Human Interaction) from Stanford University, and a bachelor’s degree from the University of California Berkeley. Rajat is the also the author of Loyalty 3.0 - How to Revolutionize Customer and Employee Engagement with Big Data and Gamification.

David Overmyer Chief Financial Officer

David Overmyer joined Bunchball in November 2012. Prior to joining Bunchball he was the CFO for Thismoment Inc from its inception through several years of rapid growth and several rounds of financing. David founded Structure Financial Services in 2007 to provide CFO and accounting services to emerging businesses. David also spent 12 years at CNET Networks (now CBSi) joining them in their start-up phase through growth to over $ 400 million in annual revenues.

Gary Chavez, Vice President, Client Services

Gary Chavez joined Bunchball in February 2011. Before joining Bunchball, he worked at Live Nation Entertainment in the Artist Services Division. During that time Gary held several senior positions including executive vice president of digital services, where he was responsible for driving digital media and marketing strategies, as well as client services, for the division. He has also held senior positions at Prophet Brand Strategy and Sony Signatures.

Erika Blaney Vice President Marketing

Branding, positioning, messaging, social media, demand generation, integrated marketing – they’re all in Erika Blaney’s wheelhouse. Erika joined Bunchball in 2013 with more than 20 years of experience and an impressive track record as a marketing strategist and management executive for B2B enterprise leaders and globally known consumer brands. Most recently, she led global corporate marketing at Axway, an enterprise integration vendor, and Tumbleweed Communications, a secure communications leader (acquired by Axway in 2009). Previous to that, she served in executive marketing posts at Cooley Godward, Certive (now C9 Analytics), and Ketchum. Throughout her career, Erika has helped shape brands like Southwest Airlines, Visa, Luna Bar and Franklin Templeton Investments.

Kenneth Jones, Vice President, Strategy and Corporate Development

Ken Jones joined Bunchball in September 2010. Prior to Bunchball Ken spent three years at Cisco in the Media Solutions Group. Ken came to Cisco in February 2007 through the acquisition of his company Five Across a white label social networking platform responsible for powering the communities of NASCAR.com and NHL.com. Ken was an early pioneer of concepts around user generated content as it related to brand management and advertising and the building of passionate online communities.

Joe Fisher, Vice President, Products

Joe joined Bunchball in June 2012.  He has more than 20 years of international product leadership leadership experience at enterprise software and enterprise security leaders.  Most recently, he was EVP of Worldwide Marketing at Axway, a leading enterprise integration vendor.  Prior to Axway, Fisher was senior vice president of product management at Tumbleweed Communications, a secure communications company  acquired by Axway.  Before that, Joe spent 4 years at Schlumberger/CellNet and held senior product positions at Furukawa Electric and IBM.

Molly Kittle, Vice President, Digital Strategy

Molly Kittle has 4+ years of gamification experience and has helped drive gamification initiatives with Bunchball clients including NBC, Warner Bros., Cisco, Hasbro, USA Network, Adobe, and ABC. In her role as VP Digital Strategy, she provides consultation for how gamification can best be used within the context of client's holistic engagement strategy. From workshops to ongoing engagement optimization, Molly is personally invested in each Bunchball client's success. Molly was recently named one of the “Top 10 Women in Gamification” by Gamification.co. Prior to Bunchball, Molly held roles in Sales and Customer Advocacy at Macrovision and ZeroG Software. Molly has a BFA in Experimental Theatre from NYU, and isn't afraid to use it.

                                  IT Solutions

                                  IT Solutions should solve business problems and drive operational efficiencies. Do yours? Our IT solution architects start with your goals in mind. We combine our IT knowledge and expertise with our extensive breadth of new and refurbished computing, networking and storage gear to craft the right solutions for your IT needs and budget.test

                                  Technology

                                  With millions of dollars worth of inventory including lines like HP 9000, Cisco routers and switches, and many others, as well as strong industry partnerships with manufacturers such as Dell, Aruba, EMC, and more, Abacus has the right technology to deliver effective solutions for all of your IT needs. We offer a broad range of refurbished products from Cisco, IBM, Sun/Oracle, HP and many others, allowing us to provide hardware at up to 50 percent off the cost of new.test

                                  Managed Services

                                  Abacus’ strategic managed services allow you to better utilize your IT budget by taking many time-consuming, day-to-day maintenance tasks off of your hands. With options for business continuity, remote datacenter management and cloud hosting available, our services will lower your overall costs while ensuring reliable access to business-critical infrastructure and data during emergencies.test

                                  Professional Services

                                  Our professional IT services are designed to help clients intelligently meet their IT goals. Abacus’ engineers are highly certified and experienced, which allows us to offer reliable auditing, solution architecting, system designing, system configuring, system installing and project managing services to clients in a wide range of industries.test

                                  Partners

Well ours did, and we're proud to say our partners are some of the best and brightest in the IT industry. But even as a trusted partner to Dell, Citrix, Nexsan and other vendors, we pledge our loyalty to our clients first.

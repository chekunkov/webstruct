If you have been looking for the best prices and service in the Dallas / Fort Worth area for your flooring or remodeling project, you have came to the right place. The Floor Barn prides itself on providing the best value to our clients for hardwood, laminate, tile, carpet & vinyl plank floors. We also specialize in bathroom and kitchen remodel projects as well as granite countertops.

 

 

The Floor Barn is owned and operated by us the Heltzel Family. Brian James Sue and Don have made improving your home and business their passion. Initially we started our little company as a remodeling and flooring installation company named Putting On The Fix. Then over the years our clients were always looking towards us to provide their materials for them. Throughout that time as well we also had rehabbed several homes and during that time we discovered that there had to be a better way to get more affordable floor materials to the public then what we encountered. After building strategic industry contacts and setting in motion a business plan to offer wholesale prices to the retail market we opened the doors of The Floor Barn to the public. The experience by our clients have been overwhelmingly positive of course who doesn’t want to save money!

 

Not only are we happy to provide discount prices to our clients, we also are very passionate about giving back to the community. Portions of each flooring sale or remodeling project, go towards a charity located within our Make A Difference Program. Not only does your shopping experience improve your home life, it also has the opportunity to improve the lives of many others in need.

 

We hope you enjoy shopping for your new Wood Tile Carpet Laminate Hardwood flooring on our website our in our store located in Burleson TX. If there is anything that we can do to make your shopping experience as best as possible please do not hesitate tocontact us.

 

We also encourage you to follow us on our Social Media pages to learn more information on our latest sales as well as trends, products and news in the flooring and remodeling industry.

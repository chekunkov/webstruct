Flooring Sales & Installation

The Floor Barn flooring store is a company that sells & install floor covering materials such as tile, hardwood, carpet, luxury vinyl plank, wood & laminate flooring. We sell Brand Name products at Discount Prices via our retail flooring stores located in Burleson and Arlington TX & we offer our installation services to customers in the entire Dallas / Fort Worth metroplex.

If you live outside our DFW service area, you can still take advantage of our low wholesale prices by shopping via our online store & we can deliver your new floors to your home.

Granite Countertops

The Floor Barn is a custom fabricator and installer of kitchen and bathroom Granite, Marble, Quartz, Silestone, Sensa Granite and other Natural Stone Countertops. Most renovation projects include Kitchen Counter tops and Bathroom Vanity Counters but we can also do granite installations for Commercial projects in addition to Residential Granite Countertops installs. Natural stone not only adds beauty to your home, but also offers high performance with low maintenance that will last for many years while adding value to your home.

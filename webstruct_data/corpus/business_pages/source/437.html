Discover a luxury hotel in Los Angeles that puts you within steps of the city’s effervescent pulse, and just as close to its cultural heart. Located in the L.A. LIVE sports and entertainment center, The Ritz-Carlton, Los Angeles offers a sophisticated haven that not only embraces the city’s glamorous vibe, but a calming retreat to escape from it all. This architecturally striking LEED® certified Los Angeles luxury hotel features 123 well-appointed guest rooms, including 14 hotel suites and the 3,000-square-foot Ritz-Carlton Suite; an elegant spa that evokes classic Hollywood; and an outdoor rooftop pool and bar that reveals breathtaking views of the L.A. skyline and Santa Monica Mountains. Home to WP24 by Wolfgang Puck, the hotel presents guests with celebrity chef modern interpretation of Chinese cuisine, along with a view as spectacular as the menu. Meetings thrive within this vibrant urban setting, along with over 100,000 square feet of event space and personalized service worthy of a red carpet event.


The Ritz-Carlton, Los Angeles is a Tier 4 property in The Ritz-Carlton Rewards.

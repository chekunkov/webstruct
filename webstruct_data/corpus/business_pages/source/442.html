Our iconic Renaissance Phoenix Downtown Hotel offers guests an artful urban retreat, combining the comfort and convenience of a full-service business and convention hotel with the stylish soul of a boutique hotel. Discover the finest in downtown Phoenix, AZ accommodations which are also convenient to the city's business and convention districts. Our Phoenix hotel's alluring colors and modern design elements welcomes travelers with Renaissance's world-class, yet localized service. Outside, the best of downtown is steps away. Walk to a Phoenix Sun's game at the US Airways Center or a Diamondback's game at Chase Field. Hop on the light-rail and head to museum district, home to the Heard Museum. Check out the Phoenix Zoo, the historic Orpheum downtown theater, or our thriving arts scene. Phoenix also has great restaurants and pubs that are sure to satisfy. When your stay is complete, Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport is conveniently close for a quick return. Welcome to the Renaissance Phoenix Downtown.

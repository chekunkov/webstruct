Choosing the Right Men’s Jeans for the Perfect Fit

Men’s jeans are a fashion lifeline for most men – they are comfortable, affordable and can look super stylish. However, if you get the wrong fitting jeans you could end up looking scruffy, disproportionate and uncomfortable. So how do you avoid these pitfalls and find the perfect men’s jeans? Follow the guide below...

Men’s jeans for the athletic figure

If you have plenty of ammunition in your guns and enjoy a good workout then your friends will have another reason to hate you, as you also have the best pick when it comes to jeans. To make the most of all those squats and press ups you need men’s jeans which skim your bottom and your thighs so classic wide legged jeans or a straight cut for more slender frames are ideal. Many men think that because they have minimum fat on their body they can slide into skinny jeans and if you have a slim athletic body then this can work, however, to avoid looking chunky stick to either straight leg or preferably wide leg men’s jeans. If you’re proud of your bottom then show it off further with detailed back pockets which are sure to bring lots of appreciating glances to your rear.

Men’s jeans for the slender figure

The biggest mistake you can make if you have a slender figure is to hide it in oversized jeans – not only is this wholly unflattering but you also risk looking like a teenage boy. If you are lucky enough to not be carrying any excess weight then you should choose jeans which enhance your figure, not hide it. The trick with slender figures is to avoid both the extremes of loose fitting jeans and skinny jeans, unless you are striving for the indie look. To truly flatter your figure, pick men’s jeans which are low rise to make the most of your stomach and that have a straight leg cut or a slight flare to help give your legs definition. For a rounder, fuller bottom, look for jeans which have detailing on the back pockets and which fit closely to your bottom rather than hanging from it.

Men’s jeans for the larger figure

If you’re carrying a little extra weight then you can still pull off the denim look – you just need to make sure you get exactly the right fit. Wide leg jeans are the best cut for this shape (avoid skinny jeans at all costs) and choose men’s jeans which have an inch or so to spare down the legs. If necessary go for a size larger than you need to avoid the jeans hugging your figure too tightly and to allow room for shrinking after washing. Large pockets which are close together on the bottom will also make your bum look smaller.

There’s no excuse for wearing men’s jeans which don’t fit, if you know you could do with a new pair to truly suit your shape then choose your men’s jeans from the great collection at Reem Clothing.

ABOUT US

Altogen Biosystems is a life sciences company that develops, markets, and manufactures cell type-specific transfection reagents. Advanced formulation of specific reagents and protocols enable efficient delivery of DNA, RNA, siRNA, and microRNA molecules. Altogen Biosystems offers a complete transfection system for a broad range of cancer cell lines and primary cells. We test all reagents for effectiveness, reproducibility, serum compatibility, and low toxicity, and all reagents can be used for both co-transfection and  in vivo experiments.

Visit Altogen Biosystems Transfection Resource: RNAi applications (siRNA transfection, microRNA transfection, plasmid DNA transfection), stable and transient transfection, delivery methods, electroporation, microinjection, non-viral and virus-mediated gene delivery, liposome and non-liposomal reagents. RNA interference (RNAi) applications (small interfering [siRNA] transfection, microRNA transfection, plasmid DNA transfection), stable and transient transfection, delivery methods, electroporation, microinjection, dendrimer-based, non-viral and virus-mediated gene delivery, liposome and non-liposomal reagents.

Altogen Labs provides specialized biotechnology and pharmaceutical services, including generating stable cell lines, RNAi services, assay development, screening, and transfection services. Because generating stably-expressing cancer cell lines and primary cells can be very expensive and time-consuming, Altogen Services offers stable cell line services by transforming the cell line of choice to express a gene of interest or short hairpin RNA (shRNA)-encoding construct. Cloning and shRNA expression services are also available.

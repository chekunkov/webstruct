About Us


Since 2003 Reemclothing.com has been revolutionising the clothes shopping experience in the UK. Shopping at Reemclothing.com lets you enjoy the convenience and cost savings associated with online shopping without compromising on the high levels of service and security you would expect from a top class high street retailer, all without leaving the comfort of your own home.

Men’s Clothing

For men, we stock a large range of the coolest items from the most fashionable designer labels in the UK, as well as some choice imports from the USA and Europe. We have all the top names in men’s fashion including Religion, Franklin and Marshall, Tommy Hilfiger Denim, Humor Jeans and Original Penguin to name just a few.

You’ll find a great selection of up to the minute Bags, Belts, Bracelets & Cufflinks, Caps & Hats, Flip Flops, Hoodies, Jackets, Jeans & Combats, Joggers, Polos, Scarves, Shirts, Shorts, Socks & Underwear, Sunglasses, Sweats & Knitwear, Swimming Shorts, Trainers & Shoes, T-shirts, Wallets, in fact, we have one of the best online ranges for men’s clothing and accessories in the UK.

Future Styles

We are constantly improving our range of men’s clothing and have new stock and labels arriving regularly, so if you can't see what you want now, keep on visiting and you'll be sure to find it soon. We also ensure that we keep a look out for up and coming designers so you can have the pick of the latest lines from the new fashion brands to ensure you stay ahead of future trends. The ranges we stock at Reem Clothing are chosen to suit individual tastes and styles so you will find everything in men’s clothing from street and pop culture influenced designs to formal looks and edgier rock and roll inspired clothing and much more.

Payment and Delivery Service

At Reem Clothing we can deliver your new clothing to you the day after you make an order, provided you order your items before 3.30pm. We operate a 14 days returns policy so if you’re not happy with your purchase you can simply send it back to us for a refund. You don’t need to worry about the security of shopping at Reemclothing.com either as our payment process is 100% secure. For more information please visit our Delivery and Returns pages.

Customer Service

We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service and want to make your shopping experience enjoyable and completely hassle free. If there is anything else you feel we can do for you or ways we can improve the clothing collections or the website then please contact us and let us know. Don’t forget to check out our Help pages for frequently asked questions and if you’re in doubt about any aspect of the ordering process just give us a call.

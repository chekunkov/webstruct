About Land Pro

Ray L. Brownfield is Managing Broker | Owner of Land Pro LLC, a full service real estate company.

Ray is a licensed Managing Broker, and provides comprehensive real estate services throughout the upper two thirds of Illinois.

Ray manages approximately 10,000 acres in Illinois and Wisconsin for a select clientele, who are absentee land owners. Ray holds the designation of Accredited Land Consultant (ALC) and Accredited Farm Manager (AFM).

Ray brings over 40 years of professional agricultural property excellence to the agriculture interests of his clients.

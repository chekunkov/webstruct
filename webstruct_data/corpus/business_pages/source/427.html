The Clarion Hotel in Federal Way is committed to providing an exceptional experience to our guest. Our staff has earned the Gold Award from Choice Hotels for outstanding guest services the past five years.

Our hotel features several amenities including: an outdoor pool, a gym, free wireless internet, cable television in each room, and a hot breakfast buffet. With 3,200 square feet available, we can accommodate conferences, weddings, and banquets.

Located between Seattle and Tacoma, our hotel is just minutes away from Sea-Tac airport and several other Puget Sound area attractions such as the Space Needle, Wild Waves Theme Park, and the King County Aquatic Center.

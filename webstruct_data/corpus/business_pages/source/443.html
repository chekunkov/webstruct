Short Pump, Virginia Hotel

Short Pump Virginia Hotel - A Perfect Mix of Business & Pleasure.

Designed for business and loaded with luxury, the Wingate by Wyndham Short Pump offers a refreshing mix of cutting-edge technology and warm Southern hospitality. Whether you’re stay is motivated by business or pleasure, you will enjoy peaceful, productive spaces, friendly service and modern lifestyle amenities for maximum comfort and productivity. Relax and stay connected in the vibrant West End of Richmond, Virginia.

Richmond Hotel Getaway.

Located next to Short Pump Town Center in Richmond’s fashionable West End, our convenient Richmond hotel is surrounded by the city’s popular shopping centers, restaurants, theaters, universities, historic landmarks, and attractions such as Richmond Coliseum, Landmark Theater, Greater Richmond Convention Center, The National Theater, Richmond International Speedway and King’s Dominion.

  • Richmond Lifestyle Lodging.

    Relax or get down to business in spacious guestrooms and beautiful suites designed for maximum comfort and capability. Our sleek Richmond accommodations feature lifestyle amenities, including work desk, cordless phone with voicemail, refrigerator, microwave, wireless Internet, 32” TVs, Neutrogena bath products, and Wyndham bedding.

  • Short Pump Hotel Destination.

    Designed for high-speed business or low-key leisure, our inviting Richmond hotel offers business and leisure travelers a productive and peaceful atmosphere. Feel inspired in our state-of-the-art meeting space, stay connected in our business center, relax in our indoor heated swimming pool and get energized in our fitness center. Fuel up your travels with a deluxe hot breakfast with your morning favorites.

  • Richmond Meeting Place.

    Whether hosting a powerhouse presentation or a family celebration, our stylish hotel is Richmond’s perfect meeting place. Get festive or fired up in over 2,200 square feet of flexible meeting space, including an executive style conference room with wireless high-speed Internet, a commercial kitchen and advanced A/V equipment. Yes, we do allow outside catering!

Clarion Hotel Federal Way

We are committed to providing a relaxing and stress-free environment. Our staff has earned the Choice Hotel's Gold Award for outstanding guest services for five consecutive years.

Address: 31611 20th Avenue South - Federal Way, WA 98003

Phone: 253.941.6000

Website: www.clarionhotelfederalway.com

The Commons at Federal Way

The Commons at Federal Way is a regional shopping mall located in Federal Way, Washington and is the only covered shopping center in the city. The current owners, Steadfast Commercial Properties, changed the name to The Commons at Federal Way.

Address: 1928 S Commons, Federal Way, WA 98003

Phone: 253.275.6008

Website: www.tcafw.com

Emerald Downs

Emerald Downs is a thoroughbred racetrack in Auburn, Washington, located a half mile east of Highway 167. It is named after Seattle, the Emerald City.

Address: 2300 Emerald Downs Dr - Auburn, WA 98001

Phone: 253.288.7000

Website: www.emeralddowns.com

Muckleshoot Casino

Muckleshoot Casino opened in April of 1995 and is owned and operated by the Muckleshoot Indian Tribe.

Address: 2402 Auburn Way S - Auburn, WA 98002

Phone: 253.804.4444

Website: www.muckleshootcasino.com

Pacific Raceways

Pacific Raceways is a mixed-use road racing facility near Kent, Washington. The race track was constructed in 1959 and opened in 1960. The track was originally named Pacific Raceways, then became known as Seattle International Raceways in 1976.

Address: 31001 144th Ave SE, Kent, WA 98042

Phone: 253.639.5927

Website: www.pacificraceways.com

Point Defiance Zoo & Aquarium

Point Defiance Zoo & Aquarium is large enough to offer an awesome array of animals yet small enough for you to get really close to them. It's fun, affordable and offers breathtaking views of Puget Sound, Mount Rainier and the Olympic Mountains.

Address: 5400 N. Pearl St. - Tacoma, WA. 98407

Phone: 253.591.5337

Website: www.pdza.org

The Outlet Collection

The Outlet Collection Seattle, formerly SuperMall of the Great Northwest, is an outlet mall in Auburn, Washington. It is anchored by Bed Bath & Beyond, Burlington Coat Factory, Marshalls, Nordstrom Rack, Old Navy, Sam's Club and VisionQuest.

Address: 1101 Supermall Way - Auburn, WA 98001

Phone: 800.729.8258

Website: www.outletcollectionseattle.com

Twin Lakes Golf Course

Rich in tradition, Twin Lakes Golf and Country Club is more than a country club. It's a home where families come together to enjoy the intangible qualities that can only be found here. Breathtaking views of Puget Sound define the panorama of our Country Club.

Address: 3583 Southwest 320th Street - Federal Way, WA 98023

Phone: 253.838.0432

Website: www.twinlakesgolf.net

Wild Waves

Wild Waves Theme Park is an amusement park in Federal Way, Washington. Opened in 1977 at The Enchanted Village, the park is a popular summer destination in the Pacific Northwest and is the largest of its kind in the area.

Address: 36201 Enchanted Pkwy S - Federal Way, WA 98003

Phone: 253.661.8000

Website: www.wildwaves.com

Celebration Park

Celebration Park. 83.5 acres; Children's play structure; Four lighted baseball fields; Four lighted soccer fields; Trails and pathways.

Address: 1095 S. 324th Street - Federal Way, WA 98003

Website: www.cityoffederalway.com/facilities.aspx?RID=1&Page=detail

Dash Point State Park

Dash Point State Park is situated on Puget Sound in Western Washington and is a popular destination for water recreation. This 398-acre camping park features 3,301 feet of saltwater shoreline.

Address: 5700 SW Dash Point Rd - Federal Way, WA 98023

Website: www.parks.wa.gov/parks/?selectedpark=Dash%20Point

King County Aquatic Center

The Weyerhaeuser King County Aquatic Center is a legacy venue of the 1990 Seattle Goodwill Games. This 2,500-seat facility maintains one of the most active competition schedules in the country, hosting more than 50 events annually.

Address: 650 SW Campus Drive - Federal Way, WA 98023

Phone: 206.296.4444

Website: www.kingcounty.gov/recreation/parks/pools.aspx

Seattle-Tacoma International Airport

The Seattle–Tacoma International Airport, also known as Sea–Tac Airport or Sea–Tac, is an American airport. It is located in SeaTac, Washington, at the intersections of State Routes 99, 509, and 518, about 1.5 miles west of Interstate 5.

Address: 17801 International Blvd - Seattle, WA 98158

Phone: 206.787.5388

Website: www.portseattle.org/sea-tac/Pages/default.aspx

ShoWare Center

The ShoWare Center is a 6,500-seat, 154,400-square-foot multi-purpose arena in Kent, Washington, United States, the construction of which was completed on January 2, 2009.

Address: 625 West James Street - Kent, WA 98032

Phone: 253.856.6777

Website: www.showarecenter.com

Tacoma Dome

The Tacoma Dome is one of the largest wood domed structures in the world. It is owned and operated by the City of Tacoma’s Public Assembly Facilities Department. Flexibility is the key to the success of the Tacoma Dome. With numerous seating configurations available.

Address: 2727 East D Street - Tacoma, WA 98421

Phone: 253.272.3663

Website: www.tacomadome.org

Auburn Academy

Established in 1919, Auburn Adventist Academy (AAA) is a private coeducational Christian boarding and day high school operated by the Seventh-day Adventist Church.

Address: 5000 Auburn Way South - Auburn, WA 98092

Phone: 253.939.5000

Website: www.auburnacademy.org

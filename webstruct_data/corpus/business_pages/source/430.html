Holiday Inn Express & Suites Canyonville

Canyonville, Oregon Hotel ... Warm Hospitality, Enchanting Scenery

Nestled in the beauty of Southern Oregon, Holiday Inn Express & Suites Canyonville is a stylish and contemporary hotel in historic Canyonville, OR. Enjoy modern, relaxed lodging within easy reach of southwest Oregon's most beautiful destinations such as Crater Lake and the Umpqua Valley wine country. Whether you are traveling for business or a family adventure, our inviting hotel offers everything you need - from hot breakfast and Wi-Fi to shuttle service - so you can relax, work, play and explore the enchanting beauty of Oregon and the Pacific Northwest.
 
Warm hospitality, modern travel amenities, and breathtaking Southern Oregon scenery define your hotel experience

A Pacific Northwest Retreat -

Touched by the natural serenity of Canyonville, Oregon, our hotel is a relaxing lifestyle retreat loaded with amenities so you are free to do your thing. Take a dip in our indoor saltwater swimming pool and hot tub. Get energized in the fitness center, stay productive in our business center and stay mobile with Wi-Fi internet access. Wake up to our generous breakfast bar, with our signature cinnamon rolls, and hop on the courtesy shuttle to Seven Feathers Casino Resort.

Discover Canyonville From Our Hotel -

From fishing to gaming to cultural destinations, you'll find it all in Canyonville, Oregon. Get into the action of Seven Feathers Casino Resort, only a half mile from the hotel, or learn the history of Oregon at the Pioneer - Indian Museum. Enjoy fishing, kayaking and whitewater rafting on the Umpqua River, explore the dramatic beauty of Crater Lake, play Myrtle Creek Golf Course, or sip your way through the Umpqua Valley Wine Country.

Southern Oregon Lodging That Feels Like Home -

Embrace the warm feeling of hospitality that radiates in all spacious guest rooms and suites. All of our beautifully appointed accommodations feature modern decor and furnishings and deluxe amenities, including flat screen HDTVs, plush luxury beds, work-friendly desks, refrigerators, microwaves, coffeemakers and voicemail. Stay connected with free Wi-Fi internet access and mix a drink at the wet bar.

Bike Locks

Any cyclist knows the importance of a sturdy, reliable bike lock. Though exact bike theft statistics are difficult to come by due to underreporting, inconsistent research, and other factors, having your bike stolen is undoubtedly a serious risk, regardless of where you live.

Unfortunately, many amateur and professional cyclists find themselves struggling to balance utility and price when shopping for bicycle security. At A&Js’ Bike Locks, we try to resolve this problem by offering a variety of the highest quality bike locks at everyday low prices.

Whether you are looking for a chain lock, U-lock, cable lock, or even an armored cable lock, A&Js’ is the place to look. We know that shopping for the right bicycle lock can be tricky, so not only do we offer a wide selection of bicycle locks from several renowned manufacturers, we help you compare the benefits of each lock using our own security rating system.

As avid cyclists ourselves, we want to give you the best service we can. Use this website to browse our complete selection of Abus, Kryptonite, and On-Guard bicycle locks, all of them offered with our low price guarantee. You can also visit our cycling community site to share your knowledge with other cycling fans.

Contact A&Js’ Bike Locks with any questions about bike locks or cycling at 708-460-2999. We’re here to help.

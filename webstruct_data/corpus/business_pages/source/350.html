From the M4’s junction 32, NW Cardiff

Exit the M4 at junction 32 and head north on the A470 towards Caerphilly and Pontypridd. After 2¼ miles, leave the A470 and take the roundabout’s 3rd exit onto the A468, heading east towards Caerphilly.

Go along the A468 dual carriageway up the hill (mind that speed camera though!) to the roundabout and go straight across (3rd exit) onto the B4600 Nantgarw Road. Go across the next roundabout and up to the traffic lights at the crossroads with St. Cenydd Road and Lon-y-Lyn. Turn right at those lights into Lon-y-Lyn.

After approximately 300 metres, turn right into Western Industrial Estate. Follow the straight road until you bear 90° left and then turn immediately right into Evans Business Centre. We are the first office on the right as you look up. Sometimes you will see a shabby looking Kwaka sat there looking a bit sorry for itself.

From J32, the journey takes about 10 minutes – depending on yer bike of course!


From the North

Heading down from the Merthyr Tydfil direction along the A470, pick up the A468 at Nantgarw and follow the directions as above.

Heading down from the Ystrad Mynach direction along the A469, when you get to the roundabout with the A468 on the north side of Caerphilly, take the third exit onto the A468.  At the next roundabout, turn left onto the B4263 St. Cenydd Road.  Head down the slight hill to the traffic lights and go straight across into Lon-y-Lyn.  After approximately 300 metres, turn right into Western Industrial Estate.  Follow the straight road until you bear 90° left and then turn immediately right into Evans Business Centre.  We are the first office on the right as you look up.  Sometimes you will see a shabby looking Kwaka sat there looking a bit sorry for itself.

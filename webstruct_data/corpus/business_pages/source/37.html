The black pepper roadshow was born in 1971, when Jim Lindsay started a mobile disco playing Motown, stax and trojan music. Since then we have done a diversify to all styles of music according to the clients wishes.

Callows Audio provides mobile discos for weddings, parties, celebrations, dinner dances and any other special event for both private and business customers.


Our mobile disco boasts a huge selection of music from the past up to the very present. Many of our customers require specific types of music from different generations. We have a wide selection of DJ's to ensure that we are able to supply the right one for your party or event.

We use many different types of professional equipment of the highest quality and ensure we supply the most suitable for your venue. The mobile disco also includes lighting and special effects if required.

Please contact us with your requirements for a personal competitive quotation.
Please note that advance booking is essential as we are a very busy mobile disco company!

"I now pronounce you husband and wife... you may kiss the bride"

Atlanta Braves RSS feed 11:15 am May 17, 2011, by Staff Writer Carroll Rogers.  Can't resist a little love in the air at the ballpark today. Cindy and Sean Logan of Suwanee tied the wedding knot today at Turner Field during batting practice. They got hitched in the stands behind the Braves dugout. The wedding ceremony was performed by Atlanta "Wedding Minister to the Stars" Reverend Thomas Johnson.

Why on a Tuesday, you ask? Apparently they had planned to get married here on Sunday May 29 but ESPN picked up the game and moved it to 8 p.m. and the minister wasn't available that night. (Hey, I thought it messed with writers plans. Ha!)

I'll attach some photos of the happy couple, who wore Bride and Groom on the back of their respective jerseys. She wore the home whites of course. Their jersey numbers (along with Cindys son Ben Letostaks) spelled out todays date 5-17-11. Nice touch!

Welcome to our online wholesale fashion jewellery store

The one stop online shop for fashion jewellery, cosmetics and accessories

We are a nationally respected wholesaler, and have been providing the highest quality fashion and costume jewellery on the market to retailers across the UK, Ireland and Europe for over 60 years!

We also stock a wide variety of wholesale cosmetics and fashion accessories, catering for all personal styles. This includes a superb range of perfume and makeup products, as well as many niche fashion products including bandanas, du rags, toe rings, pashminas, tiaras, neck ties, wedding combs and fascinators.

Buy in bulk – maximise your profits!

Our close relationships with fashion jewellery, cosmetics and clothing accessory manufacturers affords you the highest quality wholesale items around. This brings down the price at which we pass on our wholesale items to you, and alongside our buy-in-bulk service, maximises your business’ profits.

Please create a new account by clicking here, and start accessing one of the widest selections of wholesale costume and fashion jewellery, accessories and cosmetics in the UK.

A huge selection for all your customers’ tastes and budgets

Chas & Phil Wholesale also source a wide range of highly popular wholesale fashion accessories for all seasons, from warm winter hats, gloves, leg warmers and ear muffs to light and decorative satin scarves and flamboyant summery earrings.

On the left hand side of our pages, you will find the various categories of wholesale items we stock, including a dedicated cosmetics and perfume section, as well as extensive pages for wedding and hair accessories, party wear and beautiful fashion necklaces.

New wholesale products all year round

If you wish to keep up to date with our most recent wholesale products as they come in, why not check out our *NEW FOR 2013* date organised listings, which are regularly updated by our dedicated wholesale fashion jewellery specialists.

Covering the whole of the UK

Based in London, we supply our high quality wholesale fashion jewellery, perfume, accessories and cosmetics to trade customers across the UK, Ireland and Europe including London, Liverpool, Birmingham, Sheffield, Leeds, Manchester, Bradford, Glasgow, Edinburgh, Cardiff, Bristol, Swansea, Sunderland, York, Nottingham, Coventry, Dublin, Belfast, Leicester, Aberdeen, Newcastle, Peterborough, Dundee, Hull, Inverness, Plymouth, Portsmouth, Wolverhampton, Derby, Lancaster, Poole, Southampton, Canterbury, Newport, Brighton and Oxford.

We are confident in the quality of our wholesale products and our exceptional customer service, so view our online catalogue and place your order today!

Our minimum order is £65, plus £7.95 postage plus VAT. If your order is over £100, you will receive free delivery to all mainland UK addresses. There is a postage charge of £7.95 on all perfume orders to mainland UK areas. For all other areas, please call on 02085 516 580.

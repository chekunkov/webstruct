Meet The Staff

Our dedication to uncompromising quality, outstanding customer service and competitive pricing makes us your best source for your entire wood product needs. Come see us at our showroom in Rhinelander, Wisconsin, and let us be your one-stop shop for all your quality custom wood products.

Steve Ory

Owner/President

Steve Ory is the founding father of Enterprise Wood Products and along with his wife, Patti, remains the backbone of the company to this day. Steve’s hard work and determination is what has gotten the company to where it is today.

Tom Ory

Vice President

Tom Ory grew up working for his father, Steve, but went away to college not expecting to come back and work for the family company. After 4 years of college, he realized there is nothing else he would rather do. Tom hopes to build off what his father has started and continue to grow the company by bringing in some new ideas and products.

Ryan Glembin

Sales Manager

Ryan started with the company in 2011 and has helped it immensely with his sales experience and knowledge of wood products. He will do whatever it takes to find what a customer is looking for.

Eric Ory

IT Administrator

Eric is Steve's older son. He also grew up working for the family business but ventured off to college to pursue some other interests. He recently became involved with the family businesses again in May of 2012 when him and his wife, Tiffanie moved back to Rhinelander to have their first son, Nolan. The majority of Eric's time is spent at Steve's other businesss, Pioneer Equipment but he is also EWP's go to guy when it comes to computer problems.

Tiffanie Ory

Office Administrator

Tiffanie Ory is married to Steve’s other son, Eric, and joined the company in 2013. She is responsible for keeping the office running smoothly by doing a variety of tasks from invoicing to paying the bills.

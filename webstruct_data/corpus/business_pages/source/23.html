Southbound on I-5:
From either the Peace Arch Border Crossing or Truck Route 543, head south on I-5 to Exit 270. At the off-ramp stop sign turn right and enter Birch Bay Square Shopping Center. Turn onto Birch Bay Square Street to the Windermere Building. Peace Arch Business Center is on the second floor in Suite 205. We are handicap accessible and provide a elevator to the second floor.

Northbound on I-5:
Take I-5 to Exit 270. At the off-ramp stop sign turn left onto Birch Bay-Lynden Road. Proceed over the overpass to Birch Bay Square on your right. Turn onto Birch Bay Square Street to the Windermere Building. Peace Arch Business Center is on the second floor in Suite 205. We are handicap accessible and provide a elevator to the second floor.

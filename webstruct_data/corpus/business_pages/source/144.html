Testimonials

“This has been a wonderful journey with Kathleen. Our home is warm, inviting and unique.”

I had gotten to know Kathleen over the years when she was busy staging a furnishings store in San Diego that I frequented often. During one of those visits, I learned that she was going into business on her own and would be a design and staging consultant and it would be possible to hire her and use her expertise. Exciting news.

Within weeks, she was out at my home for the first time — a relatively new home with large windows and high ceilings. A lovely home but with the high ceilings and spacious rooms, I was having a difficult time placing furniture and getting used to new proportions.

We chatted on that first appointment and canvassed the house and discussed lifestyles, usage and the flow and feeling of our home. We then took off our shoes, rolled up our sleeves and got to work. It’s amazing what two women can do and what furniture they can move on their own or with a furniture dolly in a short amount of time.

My house in a matter of hours was transformed with a new flow and warmth that it had lacked, show casing many of our favorite belongings. Kathleen is all about angling pieces and although skeptical at first, I soon learned that the new angles meant more space and cohesiveness.

In the subsequent months, Kathleen has been out on several occasions to help me work on fine tuning the staging of specific rooms, hanging artwork, etc. She has also left me with a list of furniture items to possibly seek in the future and the range of measurements of those items needed to use as a guide when browsing. I carry it in the car for reference. She’s also helped in the selection of paint colors for specific rooms in the house.

This has been a wonderful journey with Kathleen our home is warm, inviting and unique. It is a reflection of our personalities, our roots and the years that we resided in foreign lands. Our home is warm, comfortable, eclectic and inviting.

Molly Hartwig

ABOUBT US:

WinPearl.com is a specialized online pearl jewelry store. We offer one of the largest selections of pearl jewelries online ranging from necklaces, earrings, bracelets, pendants, jewelry sets to loose pearls, wholesale pearls and accessories. Based in beautiful Vancouver Canada, Win Pearl was founded in 2003 by a Chinese immigrant Roger Lai who came from Canton province of China where most of the cultured pearls of the world produced. By taking the advantages of local language skill, culture background and firsthand experience of pearl farming, processing and grading, we are able to negotiate the best deal and select the best quality pearls for our customers. Through the spread of word of mouth about our good reputation, Win Pearl has been experiencing phenomenal growth even with little advertising. We have surpassed many our competitors in a relatively short period of time. At the same time, we try to balance between the business growth and customer service because we don’t want to expand our business at the cost of customer service. Giving personal touch of customer care and offering high quality products at lowest price are always our number one priority.

ABOUT OUR PRODUCTS:

We personally travel to the local pearl farms and handpick our every single pearl to ensure superior quality of our pearls. We bring all pearls back to our Canadian facilities to grade and process them by our highly trained pearl experts. We make our pearl jewelry with strict quality controls. We are not simple pearl jewelry resellers like others. We control every stage of our pearl jewelry manufacturing process from upstream pearl sourcing to downstream pearl jewelry designing or stringing. We use high quality material and accessories to make our pearl jewelries. From low priced freshwater pearls to luxury Tahitian black pearl jewelry, we always use same quality control standard. By directly buying the pearls from the pearl farms, we are able to eliminate all the cost of middle man such as importers, distributors and wholesalers. As a family owned business, our extremely efficient business management greatly reduces overhead and administration cost. Furthermore, we are a pure online business. We can fully take advantage of the power of modern technology and eliminate huge extra cost to run traditional brick and motor store. That is why we can offer same or even better quality pearls at a fraction price of regular retail store.

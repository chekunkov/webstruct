Welcome to the Parkside Whistler Resorts & Club...

Your Whistler retreat begins at the Parkside Whistler Resort and Club, offering secluded luxury at an affordable price. Spacious and bright suites are complemented by a common area hot tub with gorgeous views of Sproat Mountain and a cedar dry sauna. The picnic and barbecue facilities let you unwind after a long day on the nearby slopes. Walk to the high speed gondola at Whistler or stroll the Creekside neighborhood for all the services and amenities you desire. You have the best of both worlds. The excitement of Whistler Village is minutes away, but the tranquil neighborhood setting lets you relax.

The route to Whistler takes you directly through Vancouver and past world-renowned Stanley Park. As you pass over Lions Gate Bridge, which spans the spectacular waterway at the start of the Alaskan Cruises, you may see one of the huge ships operated by Holland America or Princess Cruises.

The journey past Howe sound, Shannon Falls and the snow covered peaks on the way to Whistler is one of Canada's most spectacular drives.... so have your cameras ready! Whistler's biggest attraction is its beautiful natural setting. Whistler Village offers all conveniences of a city just a few steps away from a pristine mountain wilderness. We are easy to reach, located just 2 hours North of Vancouver..

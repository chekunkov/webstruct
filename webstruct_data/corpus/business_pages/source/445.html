Richmond Virginia Hotel Featuring Meeting & Event Space

Looking for an intimate and inspiring venue for your next conference, convention, meeting or event in Richmond, Virginia? Located in Richmond’s dynamic West End, the Wingate by Wyndham Richmond Short Pump is the perfect destination for your all your business meetings and social events. Our relaxed, yet high-tech, hotel features 2,200 square feet of versatile event space for conventions, conferences, special events, banquets, receptions, weddings and reunions for up to 250 people. The stylish 252 square foot Conference Room offers an inspired setting for board meetings, think-tanks and small conferences. Whether hosting an intimate celebration or a powerhouse presentation, our Richmond meeting facility is an energetic and efficient meeting place in Virginia’s capitol city.

Short Pump Meeting, Event & Banquet Space.

At the Wingate Short Pump, your event, meetings and conferences come to life with high-speed Internet access in all meeting rooms, audio-visual equipment, a commercial kitchen, conference call capabilities, and a 24-hour business center. Whether working on your presentation or enjoying some down time, spacious executive style rooms and suites come loaded with modern amenities, such as WiFi, work desks, speakerphones with voicemail, TVs with HBO, sofas, microwaves, refrigerators and living rooms.  [Top]

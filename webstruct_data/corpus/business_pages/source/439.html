The Heathman - Luxury Historic Hotel in Kirkland, WA

Luxury Hotel -

Discover a place where luxury, beauty and hospitality come together in perfect harmony - The Heathman Hotel is the only upscale boutique hotel in Kirkland's vibrant downtown. Hotel delivers a refreshing blend of classic elegance, organic ambiance and modern lifestyle luxuries for a unique lodging experience just beyond the energy of Seattle, Washington. Whether visiting for a romantic getaway or a business connection, our elegant Kirkland hotel offers a peaceful, productive atmosphere for life's essential moments.

Hotel Near Seattle -

Situated just 2 blocks from Lake Washington, in charming downtown Kirkland, our elegant Kirkland hotel is just steps from downtown's arts and shopping district. Our hotel is minutes from the Seattle Waterfront, Downtown Seattle, Space Needle, Pike Place Market, Woodinville Wine Country and the Eastside's high-tech businesses. Kirkland is literally in the center of it all whether enjoying Seattle or other communities.

Luxury Hotel Accommodations -

Experience the understated elegance of our luxury hotel in Kirkland, the only Seattle community with a waterfront downtown. Our distinctive ambiance exudes natural warmth while pampering guests with gracious service and modern lifestyle amenities such as The Art of Sleep Bed menu offering your choice of 3 magnificent mattress styles, a deluxe Fitness Center, SONY HDTV's, complimentary WiFi, daily newspaper delivery, built-in work desks, multi-line speaker phones, MP3 clock radio, in-room safe, plush robes, complimentary transportation via our luxury, Hybrid SUV to any location in the central business and shopping districts of Kirkland, Bellevue, Redmond, Woodinville (airport pick-up arranged for fee). In addition, enjoy two complimentary tickets for an Argosy Cruise on Lake Washington (April - September) and see the Eastside and Seattle skyline via water!

Dining at Trellis -

Savor innovative wine-country cuisine from farm-fresh ingredients at their seasonal peak. A celebration of our land, Trellis creates a true "farm to table" dining experience. Chef Brian Scheehser fuses modern cooking techniques with local flavors and a rustic cooking style using hand-picked herbs and vegetables from his own 10-acre sustainable Farm in Woodinville. The Trellis menu is a natural masterpiece of what's growing seasonally at the Farm.

Kirkland Meeting Place -

Celebrate life's important moments at The Heathman. With 4,500 square feet of versatile event space, our luxury boutique hotel is an inspiring alternative for business meetings, conferences, receptions, banquets, social gatherings and private dinners for up to 200 people. Whether planning a romantic wedding or intimate business presentation, celebrate in an atmosphere of understated elegance that will surpass your expectations in the Greater Seattle / Kirkland destination.

Staff

Clinical Director, Lovely C. Laban GNP-C, MSN received her Masters of Nursing degree at the University of California, Los Angeles. Nationally Certified in Gerontology; she studied the aging process in depth, and applies this knowledge to treat the effects that aging has on the skin. Her Nursing Degree is from Seattle Pacific University.

Lovely’s natural ability to connect with her patients is what sets her apart from other injectors in the marketplace. It’s her love for her patients, and passion for what she does, that keeps her patients coming back. Raised on a commune outside of Seattle, Lovely grew up in a grounded family that believed that ‘natural’ is always more beautiful. It is this idea that guides Skin by Lovely’s principles, centered around getting natural-looking results that don’t leave patients looking ‘done’.

A dedicated educator, Lovely is a nationally-recognized Clinical Instructor of advanced injection techniques for aesthetic injectables and trains clinicians across North America on the use of products such as Botox and Juvederm. She’s been invited to speak at leading medical conferences such as the American Society of Plastic Surgery Nursing and the Canadian Society of Plastic Surgery Nursing. She is an expert trainer for Allergan, Inc. for Juvederm Voluma, having been one of the first clinicians in the US market to use Voluma. In this role, Lovely trains physicians and other professionals from around the U.S. on the use of this ground-breaking product. In addition, Lovely is a national trainer for Botox Cosmetic.

Lovely is the co-author of a course entitled Foundations of Clinical Skin Care, and is a frequent contributing author for beauty websites such as ‘In With Skin’ and ‘Radar Online’.

Clinical Supervisor, Marissa Abdo, MS, RN, is a Master’s-level Registered Nurse who received her Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Arizona State University, where she attended on academic scholarship and graduated Cum Laude. Marissa continued her formal studies in Europe; earning a Master of Science degree at prestigious City University in London. Marissa is a national trainer for Valeant, training clinicians from around the U.S. on the use of Restylane, Perlane, and Dysport.

Marissa held acute care positions at renowned hospitals in New York and Arizona where she developed a wide-ranging and diverse clinical background. During this time, facial aesthetics and skin care became a special area of fascination for her; later developing into a passion. She acted on this passion and joined the practice of one of Arizona’s most distinguished plastic surgeons. During this time, she gained exposure to a range of aesthetic procedures, which she provides here at Skin by Lovely™.

Marissa’s passion is discovering her patients’ unique aesthetic goals, and working with them to develop a plan to make these goals a reality. Beyond her clinical expertise, Marissa’s greatest talent is her ability to recognize each patient’s natural beauty and help them develop a plan to restore the youthful and refreshed look that we all desire. This aesthetic philosophy, combined with her warm and friendly personal style, make her an excellent addition to the Skin by Lovely™ family.

Advanced Injection Specialist, Stacey Phelps, PA-C, a compassionate health care provider. She received her undergraduate degree from UCLA in 2004 and graduated from the Master’s Level Physician Assistant program at Samuel Merritt University in 2008. During her professional career prior to joining Skin by Lovely she specialized in facial rejuvenation, dermatology, and general medicine in the San Francisco area with a recent focus on “wholistic” dermatology after returning to Los Angeles in early 2012.

With extensive education and training to match her artistic judgment, she strategically administers cosmetic injectables. In fact, Stacey honed her clinical skills with facial fillers and anti-wrinkle treatments at one of the busiest & most reputable aesthetic practices in San Francisco: under the advanced training of major manufacturers such as Allergan, Medicis, and Merz.

Board certified by the National Commission for the Certification of Physician Assistants, Stacey also maintains membership with the American Association of Physician Assistants, California Academy of Physicians Assistants, and Society of Dermatology Physician Assistants, through which she continues her education learning and applying the newest advancements in skin health and aesthetics. Stacey is committed to enhancing her patients’ natural, unique appearances through cosmetic skin treatments, as well as facilitating a graceful and natural aging process.

Dr. Behrooz A. Torkian is a Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeon dual board certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (ABFPRS), and American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery (ABO), one of only two boards qualified by the American Board of Medical Specialties (ABMS) to perform plastic and reconstructive surgery of the face. He is a member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (AAFPRS).

Dr. Torkian graduated from University of California, Los Angeles, where he graduated Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science degree in Microbiology and Molecular Genetics. He then completed his education with a Medical Doctorate at the Vanderbilt University School of Medicine. He went on to complete his specialty training in Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery at the University of California, Irvine, where he won multiple prizes in recognition of his research during residency.

Dr. Torkian then completed a fellowship in Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery at the University of California, Irvine. He is one of a select group of surgeons to have trained in a prestigious Fellowship in Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery under the auspices of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery.

Dr. Torkian served as Clinical Instructor at the University of California, Irvine in the Division of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive surgery. He continues to hold this title as Volunteer Clinical Faculty in order to teach the fellows and residents interested in Facial Plastic Surgery. He has also served as Attending Surgeon at the Veteran’s Affairs Medical Center, and at Kaiser Permanente in Orange County. Here he supervised residents and fellows in Nasal surgery including Rhinoplasty and nasal reconstruction, as well as reconstruction of skin cancer defects.

His vast experience has prepared Dr. Torkian to provide the highest level of care in Plastic Surgery of the Face and Nose. Dr. Torkian notes, “My aesthetic goals for each patient are to safely and effectively improve their features to accent their natural beauty without the obvious stigma of cosmetic surgery. My patients often tell me, ‘people cannot tell what I have had done. They say I look refreshed and well-rested – not ‘done” – the utmost compliment to patient and surgeon alike.”

Jennifer Kramer, L.E., received her esthetician license in 1993 and has been helping people improve their skin for 20 years! As a teenager, Jennifer struggled with acne and devoted the first 3 years of her career specializing in acne and ethnic skin. She continued to refine her skills by completing a highly-coveted apprenticeship with James E. Fulton, Jr. M.D., Ph.D, co-inventor of Vitamin A acid and Benzoyl Peroxide. In 1996, Jennifer opened her own clinic in Encino called Corrective Skincare (www.correctiveskincarela.com). While her business continues to thrive, she has made the decision to expand to the westside. We are honored to have Jennifer bring her wealth of knowledge and experience to the Skin by Lovely team.

Sherilyn earned her aestheticians license in 1998 and has been helping people improve their skin for more than 15 years! Aside from her passion for anti-aging skincare, Sherilyn is also an expert in treating acneic skin. After suffering with acne for 12 years, she educated herself on how to fix her active acne and is determined to share her wealth of knowledge with others. Sherilyn has been certified in numerous peels including Cosmelan and the Perfect Peel and has received specialized training and certification in DNA Stem Cell Facials.

Veronica has 10 years of experience in the spa and aesthetics industry and offers her clientele a seasoned approach to addressing their skin concerns.A thyroid cancer survivor herself; Veronica was inspired to improve her lifestyle and educate herself in the fields of preventative wellness and optimum skincare. She became certified as a Clinical Oncologist Aesthetician to work with cancer patients to restore balance in their skin and their lives. Veronica also serves as a volunteer coach for the “Look Good, Feel Better” program of the American Cancer Society.

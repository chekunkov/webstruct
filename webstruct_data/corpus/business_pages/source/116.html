Staff Bios

Owner and founder: Hugh Stearns once ran Stearns Design-Build as a one person operation and has therefore been the first employee of virtually every position in the company. He has a background in philosophy and psychology that has helped develop the design theory that drives the company’s unique approach to residential design and green building. He took his first class in green building in 1976 and has remained a student of sustainability since.

Lead Carpenter: Rod Robinson has been with the company for 9 years. In addition to being a fine carpenter, Rod also enjoys riding his Harley and volunteering for several community causes.

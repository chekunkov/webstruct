    We Design The Lab Space – And Build It To Specification  #

Welcome to Longo Laboratories.  Please browse our site and feel free to contact us with any questions.

Laboratories are complex, sophisticated and expensive workspaces. Mistakes in planning and building, or in equipment selection and installation, can be extremely costly, as well as unsafe. That’s why Longo’s experience is so valuable. An efficient laboratory, professionally designed for optimum safety and maximum functionality, furnished with precision casework, instruments and equipment, is a place where important discoveries and major breakthroughs are made.

At Longo, we take our partnership role seriously. We commit ourselves to a level of unsurpassed service which ensures that your laboratory environment will meet research specifications, address important regulatory requirements, and satisfy multiple user needs.

For almost 40 years, Longo has offered total workspace solutions for hundreds of laboratory facilities in the pharmaceutical, chemical, electronics, food science and other research-driven industries. Whether building and furnishing a bioscience lab for a major drug company or renovating a forensics facility to satisfy new regulatory guidelines, Longo can take a project from planning stage and drawings to construction and equipment installation with unparalleled expertise—and the highest level of client service.

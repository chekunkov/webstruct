Manager of Professional Services Delivery
Position: Manager of Professional Services Delivery
Location: Midwest

Information Management Technologies, Inc.® is a single-source Barcode & RFID Solution Provider, including Warehouse Management, Asset Management, Wireless Infrastructure, and Data Collection Equipment. Headquartered in Palatine, Illinois, Information Management Technologies has enjoyed explosive growth since 2001.

We are looking for a dynamic, exceptional individual to provide Management support throughout the integration process as our Manager of Professional Services Delivery. The Manager of Professional Services Delivery along with our Professional Services Department will work in conjunction to act as the main points of contact between Information Management Technologies and our clients. The Manager of Professional Services Delivery will form and manage a team that will include Systems Consultants, Project Managers, and Systems Architects ultimately to provide comprehensive solutions to our valued Clients.

In addition, the Manager of Professional Services Delivery is the person responsible for on time, on budget, per specification delivery of complex projects. The Manager of Professional Services Delivery manages our Professional Services Department to ensure Information Management Technologies meets our Client expectations and project scope throughout the implementation, ensuring the financial viability of the project and department.

Manager of Professional Services Delivery key responsibilities include:

Core Responsibilities
  • Improves internal timeline commitments for Professional Services Investment Proposals.
  • Understands what documentation needs to be created and will develop templates for missing documentation.
  • Provides professional services costs to Account Executive, Systems Architects, and System Consultants.
  • Manage the Queue of Jobs for all Professional Services Resources.
  • Develops and maintains a library of all Subcontracted Professional Services Resources.
  • Mitigates risk on all projects.
  • Accountability for Professional Services P&L Budgets.
  • Reports to the President.

Tucson Downtown Hotel The Riverpark Inn -- The Hotel Only Hotel with a Streetcar Stop!

Our Downtown Location. 

The Arizona Riverpark Inn is a full service, AAA 3 diamond hotel in a resort-like setting.  With Sun Link Streetcar service scheduled to begin July 2014 you will be able to travel from the hotel with comfort and ease through downtown to 4th Avenue, the University of Arizona and the Medical Center.  Experience the economic boom in downtown Tucson while discovering the things that make our growing and vibrant desert community unique -- new restaurants, entertainment, offices and housing.  With 174 rooms, (including five suites) on two floors, we have everything you need to make your stay in Tucson not just comfortable, but the kind of luxurious experience that will make you want to come and visit again. The Arizona Riverpark Inn is a proud corporate sponsor of the University of Arizona Campus Recreation Department and the University's Club Sports Program. We extend a warm welcome to the former guests of the Hotel Arizona.   

 

 

The Arizona Riverpark Inn offers shuttle service within a 3-mile radius of the hotel.  Regular shuttle hours are Monday through Friday from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.  Please note that 72-hour advance reservations are required/shuttle service is subject to availability and service may not be available during certain special events.  Shuttle service for groups/conventions is subject to negotiation. We are just a short drive from a panorama of area attractions including the University of Arizona, Reid Park Zoo, Arizona Sonora Desert Museum, Old Tucson Studios, San Xavier Mission, Sabino Canyon, Pima Air & Space Museum, Saguaro National Park and some of the best golf you’ll ever play. We offer discount specials and packages.

  • Hotel With Free WiFi.

    Our deluxe guest rooms offer a choice of either one standard King size bed or two Queen size beds.  We also offer a King Courtyard View Mini Suite which includes one King size bed plus sofa bed. All rooms feature free high-speed internet access, two line speaker telephone with voicemail, Direct TV, microwave, refrigerator, in-room safe, hair dryer, iron and ironing board, and in-room coffee. Enjoy a game of tennis* or relax in our outdoor heated pool and Jacuzzi surrounded by lush landscaping and mountain views. 

  • Tucson Meetings and Events.

    The Arizona Riverpark Inn has 14,244 square feet of meeting and event space comprised of seven rooms and outdoor venues which can accommodate up to 500 guests for banquets and up to 2,000 guests for receptions. Our Rio Nuevo Ballroom, with 16-foot high ceilings, is the perfect venue for any business or social event.

  • Hotel Amenities.

    The Arizona Riverpark Inn's FREE Full American Breakfast* is a real crowd-pleaser!  We offer a delicious choice of scrambled eggs, breakfast meat, breakfast potatoes, made-to-order waffles, cold and hot cereals, pastries, toast, bagels, juice, coffee and tea.  Including in-room amenities such as refrigerator and microwave, Direct TV and free high-speed Internet, we also offer services guaranteed to make you feel not just comfortable, but pampered. 
  • Tucson’s 350+ days of sunshine makes our area ideal for outdoor activities. The Arizona Riverpark Inn has an impressive list of sports and fitness features including tennis court, paddle tennis, shuffleboard, biking paths, jogging paths, bocce ball, croquet, and fitness center*. (*Please note that our Complimentary Breakfast and offered amenities are subject to seasonal restrictions and may not be included in some negotiated group rates.  The Complimentary Breakfast is not offered during the Pueblo Gem & Mineral Show -- January 23 through February 17, 2015.)       

  • Local Dining.

    Tucson is fast earning a reputation as a culinary trendsetter, which is redefining local cuisine. The Arizona Riverpark Inn is pleased to have one of the best restaurants in downtown, the Terrace Café, on-site. The Terrace Café overlooking the courtyard and pool at the Arizona Riverpark Inn Hotel provides excellent eclectic pub cuisine in a comfortable resort-like setting in Downtown Tucson for a reasonably priced casual meal. Join us in the adjacent Lobby Bar.  Be sure to ask our bartender about the daily drink specials -- no visit would be complete without sampling one of our Margaritas!   Check with staff at the hotel registration desk for opening and closing hours.  There are a wide variety of dining experiences available within a mile of the Riverpark Inn.

  • Shopping in Tucson.  

    Visitors and residents alike recognize that Tucson offers plenty of possibilities for terrific shopping excursions making it ideal for shopping enthusiasts.  With expansive outdoor malls, shopping villages, small trendy specialty designer boutiques, art galleries, antiques and collectible shops, bookstores, and museum shops Tucson is ideal for visitors who find shopping on the top of their list of things to do. Book your stay at the Arizona Riverpark Inn and explore Tucson's multi-cultural global marketplace.  Shops with an assortment of merchandise tempt the savvy consumer with something for every age, taste and budget.  You will find it all at Tucson Spectrum, Irvington Plaza, Tucson Mall, Park Place, Casa Adobes Plaza, Plaza Palomino, La Encantada, Plaza Colonial and Ventana Plaza as well as other independent shops and venues.

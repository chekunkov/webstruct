BRING YOUR DREAMS TO LIFE

WITH AN AWARD-WINNING TEAM of Contractors

A  track record of contractor success in North Vancouver and West Vancouver

Upward Construction & Renovation has an established track record in custom home builds, major renovations and remodeling type projects in North Vancouver and West Vancouver. Along with our site personnel, we have experienced in-office project management and coordination for each project, ensuring that all members of the project team and all of our subcontractors are working towards the realization of your goals and ensuring budgets are met and quality is achieved.

Built on strong relationships

We have loyal and long standing relationships with many of our sub trades, giving us prompt and quality service, making it easier to deliver homes on time and on budget. We treat all our employees and subcontractors equally and fairly, expecting the same in return. From this loyalty, our clients will benefit and it creates positive environment and experience for all.

Bonded and Insured

We promote site safety on all our projects and ensure that all of our employees and subcontractors are aware of our site safety procedures. We have WorkSafe BC insurance and ensure that any subcontractors on site are covered and have their own General Liability insurance.

MICHAEL UPWARD

OWNER, PRINCIPAL

Read my Bio
[x] close

Michael graduated from Auckland Technical Institute in 1980, and moved to Canada shortly thereafter. Initially partnering and managing a couple of different businesses, he decided in 2002 to satisfy his love of building by creating Upward Construction & Renovation.

Michael deliberately started out small, initially taking on only renovations to ensure that excellent service and attentiveness was provided.  His reputation as a builder with integrity resulted in so much business that within a year he expanded by bringing in an experienced team to assist in achieving and maintaining the quality and craftsmanship on every project.    Today Upward Construction & Renovation is one of the top five builders and renovators on Vancouver’s North Shore and has been profiled in many magazines. Projects range from $500,000.00 to in excess of $3,000,000.00, ranging from large renovations to new custom homes. Michael is an active member of RenoMark and Greater Vancouver Home Builder Association and has received multiple accolades for several Upward homes, being finalists for the CHBA National SAM Awards and the Georgie Awards.

While the company now has a team of more than twenty employees, it retains the personal touch that is at the heart of all Michael’s business relationships. 

Michael resides in West Vancouver with his wife, Tammy, and two grown children.


JUHLI MILLS

EMMA CARTER

INTERIOR DESIGNER

DAN MARTIN

SITE SUPERINTENDENT

[x] close

Born and raised in Ontario, Dan moved to Vancouver five years ago and is an inter-provincially licensed carpenter.  With more than 13 years' experience in commercial and residential construction his extensive involvement in "start to finish" builds have resulted in a greater understanding of the entire building process, as well as all of the trades' roles and responsibilities. Michael Lane

Account Manager

Read my Bio
[x] close

With over 30 years of experience in the construction, real estate and development, Michael started off first in sales of wholesale lumber and then acted as a general contractor and management consultant in home construction and design, which involved the construction of many fine quality custom homes and various home renovations. Also for many years, Michael was a realtor working on the North Shore with many developers and builders facilitating the entire development process from procurement of building lots, home design for the target market, and to selling the finished product. Michael’s role at Upward entails the prospecting of new clients in advising and building their dream home or renovation project.

Born in Vancouver, Michael was raised on the North Shore.  He resides in West Vancouver with his partner and teenage kids. Michael’s hobbies include biking, running and tennis, completing a few races every year.  You will often find him on the Grouse grind or trekking up Cypress on his bike.

        The Apartment

        The meetings and events space like no other.

        Make yourself at home and raid the Pantry stocked full of free drinks and goodies, whilst relaxing or getting some work done in the six individually designed rooms. Alternatively make use of the whole apartment for a standing event up to 250 guests.

        By Day: Ideal for meetings, brainstorming sessions, presentations, team catch ups or those Friday 'finish-early-go-to-the-pub' meetings.

        By Night: The Hippest destination for exclusive events and private parties when the sun goes down.

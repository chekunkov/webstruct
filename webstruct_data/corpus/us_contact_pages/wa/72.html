  • Donation Requests

    Corner Bakery Cafe is committed to giving back to our neighborhoods and communities. To request a donation for a community event or school night fundraiser, please submit your request following these guidelines:

    • All requests must be submitted in writing by regular mail or e-mail.
    • We do not accept requests or inquiries by phone or fax.
    • Address: Corner Bakery Cafe Donations
                     12700 Park Central Drive, Suite 1300
                     Dallas, TX 75251
    • E-mail address: donations[at]cornerbakerycafe[dot]com
    • Requests must be submitted on your organization’s official letterhead.
    • Please include the following: information about your organization, proof of non-profit status, date of event, and location of event (city and state).
    • Requests must be received at least 60 days in advance of your event to be considered.
    • Donation requests are only accepted for eligible non-profits, schools, and community organizations.

    All requests for donations meeting these guidelines will be reviewed on an ongoing basis. If your request is approved, we will contact you no later than 30 days prior to your event. Thank you for giving us the opportunity to review your request!
